Why are they cool?

Sea otters are like the ultimate seafood enthusiasts of the ocean, with a taste for the finest underwater delicacies. They're basically the gourmets of the sea, munching on everything from crunchy crabs to succulent clams, slurping down sea urchins like they're oyster shooters, and savoring mussels like they're at a fancy underwater buffet.
